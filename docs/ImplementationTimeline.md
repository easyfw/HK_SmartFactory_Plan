## Critical Path Items

1. **Network Infrastructure** (Months 1-3)
   - Dependencies: All subsequent deployments
   - Risk: High - delays impact entire project

2. **MES Implementation** (Months 3-6)
   - Dependencies: IoT scale-up, analytics
   - Risk: High - core system for operations

3. **IoT Deployment** (Months 4-8)
   - Dependencies: Predictive maintenance, digital twin
   - Risk: Medium - phased approach reduces risk

4. **Digital Twin Development** (Months 13-22)
   - Dependencies: IoT data, MES integration
   - Risk: Medium - complex but well-planned

5. **Advanced Automation** (Months 19-24)
   - Dependencies: Digital twin, optimization
   - Risk: Medium - final phase integration
